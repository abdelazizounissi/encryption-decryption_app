Monoalphabetic Cipher

A substitution cipher where each letter is replaced with another fixed letter. The same plaintext letter always encrypts to the same ciphertext letter.

Caesar Cipher

A shift cipher where each letter is shifted by a fixed number of positions in the alphabet. Also known as a ROT cipher.
